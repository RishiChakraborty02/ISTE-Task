About Us
Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national, non-profit making society registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860. Our aim is to equip engineers of today with the technical and management skills to solve the challenges of tomorrow by learning and being a part of departments such as technical , electrical, management and design. We also organize a plethora of events and hands on workshops aimed at making better professionals, bringing about holistic development of our community.

Domains

Management

Develop astute managerial skills to publicize, promote and manage a world full of wonderful events! Enrapture the audience with your pitching skills and learn what it takes to make an event successful.

Design

The place to master the core design principles and breathe life into your ideas by getting in sync with your creative side. Create captivating designs to dazzle the crowd!

Technical

Hands-on learning is the best way to detect, analyze and develop solutions to real life problems. Hone your skills in the Electrical and CSE domains by exploring various fields like Web Development, App Development, Machine learning, building hardware projects and a lot more!
